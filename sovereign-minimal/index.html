Powerhouse is a deliberate space examining how power moves through desire, how intimacy creates consequence, and how choices shape the self. No voyeurism. No moral instruction. Just inquiry.

Core Thesis

"Power is the invisible architecture of most relationships. It determines who gets to want without cost, and who must negotiate for safety."

We are a team of writers, editors, and thinkers who believe that many of the most formative moments in adult life happen in private. We believe that relationships are infrastructure. They shape how people move through the world, how they endure, or how they fracture under pressure.
